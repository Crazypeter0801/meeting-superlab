# Tencent Meeting Design Quality Checklist

## Product

- The primary user role is clear.
- The meeting stage is clear: before, during, after, cross-device, or admin.
- The main action is visible within 10 seconds.
- The design does not add unnecessary steps during an active meeting.
- Host and participant permissions are not confused.

## Trust And Privacy

- Recording status is explicit when relevant.
- AI summary or transcript visibility is explicit.
- External guest presence is visible when relevant.
- Camera and mic permission states are clear.
- Admin policy blocks explain what happened and what the user can do.

## Visual System

- Neutral foundations carry most of the UI.
- Blue is reserved for primary actions and active states.
- Purple or AI accent is used only for AI-specific surfaces.
- Cards are not nested inside cards.
- Radius and shadow are restrained.
- Typography scale is limited and readable.
- Spacing inside groups is tighter than spacing between groups.

## Mobile Ergonomics

- Safe areas are respected.
- Touch targets are at least 44 x 44.
- Bottom controls are reachable.
- Text does not overflow controls or chips.
- Sheets can handle long content.
- Critical actions are not hidden behind ambiguous menus.

## States

- Normal state.
- Loading or generating state.
- Empty state.
- Error or unavailable state.
- Permission denied state.
- Weak network or reconnecting state.
- Role-limited state.

## Review Readiness

- Frames are named clearly.
- Data examples are realistic.
- Design assumptions are labeled.
- Missing evidence is listed.
- The next decision needed from reviewers is explicit.
